# Tasty Recipes

Fully responsive app about food recipes and desserts from all around the world. A place where you can find ideas for your daily meals and also, by authenticating through its secure ways (JWT & Bcrypt), you will receive access to add favorites/bookmarks and to create/edit and share your own recipes.

If you are interested in just taking a short tour of the app and experience all its functionalities but without authenticating, then don't worry, a test account is set up for you in the Login section!
